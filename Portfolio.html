TRAVEL APP

Travel App is an app allowing you to find your way from one place to another using combined transport. This app is based on ©Rome2Rio, a multimodal transport search engine.
Find different transport combinations - anywhere in the world, from the cheapest option to the quickest.
  • Price - See an indicative price for every route in any currency
  • Complete flight informations - flights timetables are available and, most of time, some additionnal information       like departure and arrival terminals, types of planes...
  • Frequency - Frequency of buses, trains, ferries...
  • Complete itinerary - Shows you where you need to get off and on the next means of transportation.
  • Gps - To guide you to the next stop.
  • Offline searches - Your last 50 searches are saved and can be displayed offline.
